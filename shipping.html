SHIPPING

After Checkout, once the order is confirmed we will send yo a notification via email as soon as the item is shipped with the tracking details. Shipping & Handling will take approximately 3-4 business days or sooner. (Please note these do not include weekends & Holidays)

Most products are in-stock while special & new items are pre-order. Certain restrictions prevent us from shipping certain products to all geographical locations. Orders will be fulfilled in the order they are received.

We use DTDC / Bluedart or other shipping carriers for domestic orders (India) & FedEx or other carriers for international orders (Worldwide). After receiving an order, we will send you an e-mail acknowledging that we have received your order. ("Order Confirmation"). Customers are responsible for customs, taxes and duties for worldwide orders.
