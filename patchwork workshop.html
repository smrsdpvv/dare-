ACCOUNTS CART

Clothing is one of the best ways to show off your personality and we at dare believe that owning up to your authentic self comes with a lot of acceptance and confidence.

From our previous ‘make your own t-shirt’ workshops, we saw how people showed love putting together a design on their own. To give a similar experience we put together a Patchworking + Painting workshop.

Patchworking is the craft of sewing in small pieces of cloth in different designs, colours, or textures sewn together.

So, we put together a few different patches, some painted and some for people to paint on. With our friend, Ann, helping out on the sewing part the whole workshop became an amazing experiment.
