EXCHANGES & RETURNS

To initiate an exchange, please contact our customer service team at info@darestudio.com within 5 days of receiving the item.

Catering to the hygiene standards for our products; all the exchanges received will go through a quality check by our team and would be further approved/declined for exchange

We deserve the right to not accept a return; if the products(s) are returned after use and if the product(s) are damaged.

The exchange once confirmed has to be shipped by the customer with the tracking ID sent to us on info@darestudio.com on the same email thread. Once we receive the returned item, we will process the exchange within 7-10 business days

If you have received a defective product, share images at info@darestudio.com and we will get back to you within 24 hours.

Exchanges are subject to availability, and we cannot guarantee that the size you need will be in stock. If the size you need is not available, we will issue you a store credit. The store credit is valid till 1 month from the date of issue.
