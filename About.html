DARE est.2022

Dare is an art and art experience brand. At Dare, we're passionate about art and believe that it's more than just a visual experience. We see art as a powerful medium for communication and self-expression.

Our collections feature designs inspired by various art forms, including paintings, culture, and literature. Each piece is crafted carefully to reflect and convey an idea or a thought.

We focus on creating a supportive atmosphere that inspires creativity and promotes self-expression through our experiences and products.

This is a passion project of us, Co-founders - Varadharajan and Roshini. Our belief is rooted in an idea that anything and everything is art. We constantly work on putting together ways beyond traditional methods to bring art to you.
